About Us





Our mission for creating this website is reducing the cancer mortality rate by focusing individual behaviors, public policy and discussion, and research on prevention and early detection Addressing all preventable cancers across all populations. Making prevention knowledge and early detection services more accessible for people in medically underserved communities Collaborating with partners to maximize the effectiveness and reach of the prevention and early detection programs and message Creating an energizing and productive workplace that people want to join.
